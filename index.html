Junior Program

Our junior program lays the groundwork for aspiring young players by focusing on fundamental skills. From stickhandling and passing to shooting techniques, we provide a comprehensive curriculum designed to build a strong foundation.

Teenager Program

Building upon foundational skills, our program places a significant emphasis on advanced skill refinement. From tactical strategies to position-specific training, teenagers undergo a comprehensive curriculum designed to elevate their game to new heights.

Professional Program

This program is crafted for players who have already honed their foundational skills. We focus on advanced skill refinement, elevating techniques in areas such as stickhandling, precision passing, strategic shooting, and tactical game intelligence.Led by a coaching team comprised of seasoned professionals and experts in the field, our program benefits from a wealth of knowledge. This mentorship is not just about refining skills but also navigating the complex nuances of high-stakes professional play.
